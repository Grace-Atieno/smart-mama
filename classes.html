Our Classes

Smart Mama offers empowering classes designed to guide you through every stage of pregnancy and birth — whether you prefer online convenience or face-to-face sessions.

Lamaze Classes

Learn proven techniques to manage labor through focused breathing, movement, and relaxation. Available both online and in-person.

Pregnancy Education

Weekly sessions covering fetal development, nutrition, mental wellness, and preparing for childbirth.

Postpartum Support

Guidance for new moms on healing, breastfeeding, and mental health — because care continues after birth.
